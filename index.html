About us

Acumelt Pvt. Ltd. is a Ductile Iron casting foundry based in Rajkot. We at Acumelt have adopted flaskless high pressure molding for greensand mold making. We are equipped with the latest technology required for all production processes including but not limited to sand preparation, melting, pouring, core making, quality assurance etc.

Acumelt is endowed with unmatched managerial expertise, financial endowment, immense domestic support and over 50 years of experience in the manufacturing sector. We at Acumelt have been blessed with numerous human and managerial resources which have helped us acquire not just state of the art physical infrastructure, but also highly experienced and capable personnel from across the country.

We are committed to making high quality ductile iron castings and to ensure a healthy, sustainable and socially responsible growth for our organization for the benefit of all relevant stakeholders.
